ABOUT THE HATCHERY





The Kosgoda turtle hatchery has been one of the turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka that has played a major role in nurturing and preserving the lives of sea turtle. As we all know, sea turtles in Sri Lanka and as well as all around the world are in danger of poachers and other several external threats discussed in this website. The Kosgoda turtle hatchery is also a place that eductes its visitors on the different sea turtle population in Sri Lanka as well as the difficulties they face at sea. This hatchery has been existing since 40 years ago and is still operating and running up till today.






Few facts about the hatchery

1. This hatchery started almost 40 years ago in Kosgoda.

2. Kosgoda is a town in the Galle district.

3. They play a huge role in conserving the lives of turtles.

4. The people working in the hatchery will provide you information on the wellbeings of the turtle and the threats they face in Sri Lanka.

5. They have 5 kinds of turtles in their hatchery.

Your experience at the hatchery

1. You can witness the hatching of the hatchlings.

2. Learn about the 5 kinds of turtles in the hatchery.

3. You can release the hatchlings into the ocean with your family. It will be a memory to remember.

4. Experience the happiness of seeing the beautiful sunset at our hatchery.

5. It will surely be an experience to cherish and remember!

Your contribution to this hatchery

1. The sea turtles in Sri Lanka are currently declining in population and are endangered. So the roles played by the workers at the hatchery and you, the visitors is important in nurturing and preserving the lives of our turtles.

2. There are so many things you could do to help the hatchery. A few options are listed in the Purchase and Donate page.

3. Since most hatcheries buy the eggs from from poachers at a high price with the intention to save their lives and let them into the ocean once they're able to, it'll be of huge help to these turtle hatcheries.
